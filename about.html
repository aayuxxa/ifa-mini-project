About Us

Our Mission

To influence market forces through vibrant and sustainable agricuiture activity that reduces poverty, reduces the risk of food insecurity and increases the contribution to our farmers day to day life. To encourage our Children to value agriculture and to appreciate the importance of naturally grown and harvested foods.

Our Values

Our values are driven with purpose to create and build a business of authentic foods. And to spread our passion for food to as many people as we can while staying true and honest to its roots.

Our Vision

To lead and manage the delivery of appropriate services to enable previously disadvantaged commercial farmers to achieve sustainable success in the agribusiness sector through partnerships and knowledge.
